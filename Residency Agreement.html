RMU HOSTEL AGREEMENT

THE ACCOMODATION RESIDENT HEREBY AGREES TO THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:


REFUND POLICY


  1. 1. Refund request within Seven (7) days of payment, 100% refund on paid amount.
  2. 2. Refund request Seven (7) days after payment, 70% refund on the paid amount.
  3. 3. There will be NO REFUND on any payment made two (2) weeks prior to reopening date.
  4. 4. All Refunds can ONLY be claimed or processed by the Student name on payment receipt.
  5. 5. To use the room allocated for residential purposes only.

NOTE:The date of receipt of request form shall be treated as date of request.


    PROCEDURE FOR REFUND

  1. 1. Fill in person a student refund request form at the hostel administration with a scan copy of your student ID card.
  2. 2. If your refund is approved, we will contact you through an SMS with date and procedure for collection.

RULES AND REGULATIONS
  1. 1. Students admitted into the hostel are required to be in residence during the whole of the academic year. In extreme situations an early withdrawal will result in a percentage loss of paid rent by refund.
  2. 2. Students who are in debt to the hostel or who have not paid the appropriate residence fees in advance will (at the time of reporting) not be allowed to come into residence.
  3. 3. Students will only be allowed into allocated rooms only upon full payment of residential fees.
  4. 4. Students are to vacate the rooms when the university is on recess unless permitted by the absolute discretion of the hostel subject to payment in full of imposed fees.
  5. 5. The hostel reserves the right to relocate students to a different room of same type.
  6. 6. Under no circumstances shall a student sell or transfer rooms allocated to him/ her. Mutual exchange of rooms after final allotment is not allowed. However, only hostel admission office may allow as a special case on valid and reasonable grounds.
